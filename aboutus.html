About Us

Travelling Birds: Unveiling the World, Wing by Wing

Welcome to Travelling Birds, your passport to unforgettable journeys and unparalleled adventures. Nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, we spread our wings to help you explore the world in ways you've only dreamed of. With a passion for travel and a deep-rooted love for discovery, we are more than a travel agency – we are your companions in crafting memories that will forever soar.


Our Feathered Philosophy:

At Travelling Birds, we believe that every journey is an opportunity for transformation. Guided by this philosophy, we curate experiences that go beyond the conventional and delve into the essence of each destination. Our team of avid travelers and seasoned professionals work tirelessly to design itineraries that not only showcase the must-see landmarks but also uncover the hidden gems, local flavors, and authentic cultures that make each place unique.


Why Choose Us?

Personalized Attention: Just as each bird has distinct plumage, every traveler has unique preferences. We take the time to understand your desires, interests, and dreams to tailor-make journeys that resonate with you on a personal level.

Local Expertise: Our wingspan extends across the globe, but our roots are firmly planted in Nepal. With an intricate understanding of the local nuances and a network of trusted connections worldwide, we provide you with insights and access that only a local can offer.

Sustainable Wanderlust: We believe in responsible tourism that leaves a positive impact on the places we visit. Our itineraries are designed to promote sustainable practices, support local communities, and protect the environment.

Seamless Exploration: Leave the logistics to us. From flights and accommodations to transfers and activities, we handle every detail so you can focus on savoring every moment of your journey.

24/7 Support: No matter where you are in the world, we're just a call away. Our round-the-clock support ensures that you're never alone during your travels.
